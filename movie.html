The Endless

Reign of destruction.

Official Release Date: March 15, 2026

About the Movie

After being imprisoned for a century by occultists, Dream escapes and must reclaim his stolen tools of power to restore order to his realm, The Dreaming. However, his brother Destruction has other plans, seeking to destroy the fabric of reality itself. As Dream confronts his past and battles his brother's destructive ambitions, he must also navigate the complex relationships with his siblings, including Death, who holds the key to the balance of life and death. The Endless is a visually stunning and emotionally gripping tale of family, power, and the eternal struggle between creation and destruction.

Cast

  • Dream - A sibling of destruction, and an immortal who lives in the dreaming. He is the universe's guardian of dreams and nightmares and its only hope of saving it, for what is a world without dreams?

  • Destruction - One of the seven immortals who seeks to destroy the timeline.

  • Death - She is the sister of Dream and Destruction, and the guardian of the end of all things. She is a powerful force that brings about the conclusion of cycles and realities and destruction's accomplice, for when ever there's destruction, there must be death.
