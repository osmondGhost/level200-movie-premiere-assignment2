Shadows of Eternity

Where past and future collide in an endless night.

Official Release Date: March 15, 2026

About the Movie

In the year 2147, humanity has colonized distant planets, but the ancient ruins of an extinct alien race hold secrets that could unravel the fabric of time itself. Dr. Elara Voss, a brilliant chronologist, discovers a mysterious artifact that allows glimpses into alternate realities. As she delves deeper, she realizes that her actions are being manipulated by shadowy figures from a parallel dimension who seek to erase her timeline to prevent a catastrophic event. Joined by a rogue AI companion and a skeptical historian, Elara must navigate through fractured realities, battling temporal anomalies and her own doubts. The story explores themes of identity, destiny, and the consequences of tampering with time, culminating in a breathtaking climax where the boundaries between past, present, and future blur into oblivion. With stunning visual effects and a gripping narrative, Shadows of Eternity challenges viewers to question the nature of existence and the price of knowledge.

Cast

  • Dr. Elara Voss - A determined chronologist who uncovers the artifact and leads the quest to save her reality.
  • Orion - A sentient AI with a rebellious streak, providing comic relief and crucial technological support.
  • Professor Harlan Reed - A wise historian skeptical of time travel, who becomes an unlikely ally in the fight against temporal chaos.
